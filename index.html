Rutgers WiCS

Rutgers Women in Computer Science is dedicated to the advancement of women in CS. Through our mentorship program and community events, we are able to build a supportive community, promote educational and professional development, and empower our members to overcome societal barriers.

     

HackHERS is the annual, women-centric hackathon at Rutgers University. We aim to create a space in which women can explore tech culture and be empowered to create with code.

Part of a multi-department initiative to improve the experience for female-identifying computer science majors at Rutgers, was born from a desire to share Douglass resources with the larger University community. As a peer mentor, I will act as community-builders and community leaders. Peer mentors will serve as a starting point for a better experience for students who are underrepresented in the computer science major at Rutgers.
